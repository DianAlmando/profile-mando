What is Automation Engineering ?

About Automation Engineering
Automation Engineering is the basic substance of Electronics Engineering with specialization in Automation Engineering. Automation Engineering is an engineering field that focuses on the design, development, implementation, and maintenance of automated systems. Automation systems include the use of software and automated equipment to control and monitor production processes, operations, and other systems.
